# Observed Brand Patterns

These notes summarize the recurring presentation-language found in the deidentified Nexon report decks under `.vscode/`.

## Confirmed Theme Traits

- Slide size: `12192000 x 6858000` in `ppt/presentation.xml` for all reviewed decks.
- Theme font: `Arial` in `ppt/theme/theme1.xml`.
- Repeated theme colors from `ppt/theme/theme1.xml`:
  - text/dark neutral: `#000000` and `#1D1D1D`
  - background/light neutral: `#FFFFFF` and `#F7F1F1`
  - accent 1: `#2E8AF4`
  - accent 2: `#E34749`
  - accent 3: `#FFBF00`
  - accent 4: `#00C982`
  - accent 5: `#87C9F7`
  - accent 6: `#FA9EAB`
  - hyperlink green: `#00C982`

## Repeated Slide Behaviors

- Cover slide pattern:
  - presenter line
  - customer name
  - `Monthly Service Review`
  - month and year
- Body-slide pattern:
  - direct headline
  - one core evidence block
  - short commentary on exception-heavy or trend-heavy slides
- Many slides repeat the confidentiality footer:
  - `Copyright © 2026 Nexon Asia Pacific. All rights reserved. Commercial In Confidence.`
- Section changes are often handled by simple divider/title slides rather than complex transitions.
- Dense operational tables are allowed, but usually split across sequential continuation slides.

## Practical Implications

- Start from a restrained corporate theme, not a design-showcase theme.
- Build consistency by repeating title position, footer treatment, and commentary styling.
- Let charts and tables carry most of the visual weight.
- Reserve bright accents for meaning, not decoration.
